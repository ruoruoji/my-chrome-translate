Language learning can be much easier when translation and pronunciation are just one double click away. Instead of opening a new tab or copying text into another tool, you can simply stay on the current page and focus on what you are reading.

Try double-clicking on words like pronunciation, confidence, or even a short phrase such as language learning. For single words, you should also see the phonetic transcription and an extra audio source from the dictionary.

This demo page is purely static and does not contain any additional scripts, so it is a good place to verify that the extension behaves well on a clean website.
